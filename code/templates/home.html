Stream Hopper

About Us

Keeping up with which streaming service has the content you want is hard. Finding which streaming service is best suited to your own taste is even harder!

With the oversaturated market of streaming services, Stream Hopper is a product that was designed to help consumers, like you, figure out which streaming service you can drop for the month and which one you should subscribe to or keep based on your personal preference.

We will provide recommendations of which streaming service contains the best content for a user's taste. This way our users can maximize their bingeing satisfaction with an optimal single subscription fee.
