Why StreamHopper?

Watching tv series or movies online in 2020 can be very stressful. There are dozens of streaming service options, each with constantly changing catalogs of thousands of shows. According to a 2019 Deloitte survey, 47% of consumers overwhelmed with the number of streaming services available to them. 57% have reported shows they like suddenly "disappearing" from their service due to rights expiring.


StreamHopper provides a solution using a blend of subject matter expertise and machine learning algorithms. Just fill out a 3-minute questionnaire and StreamHopper will recommend the best service for you, updated every month. Currently, our model keeps track of the complete active catalogs of 7 streaming services, with over 7,000 series and 11,000 movies. StreamHopper will minimize the burden of choosing a service, while maximizing the content you get to see.

Since our launch in March 2020, we have helped many streamers to find the content they like and choose the service they deserve.
